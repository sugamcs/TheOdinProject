Chai(tea)

Description

Chai is the classic indian tea which is loved around the globe.


Ingredients

  • Tea leaves or any local tea brand
  • Add half to full glass of water to the pan for every individual
  • Add half glass of milk for every individual
  • 2 tea spoon of sugar for every individual
  • Add your choice of masala(cardamom,cloves, etc)

Steps

  1. Put the tea leaves in the boiling water and let it get bubbled
  2. Add the milk along with desired masalas
  3. Let it boil and your tea is ready!
