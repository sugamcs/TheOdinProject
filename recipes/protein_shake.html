This is a protein shake

Description

A protein shake is an easy-to-make recipe that is nutrious nad healthy

It is really easy to drink it on the go and could be a lifesaver for gym goers who need to complete their daily protein intake to build muscle and lose fat


Ingredients

  • ice cubes
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 scope chocolate flavoured whey protein
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter/li>
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Make sure you are ready with all the ingredients
  2. Blend ice cubes, milk, banana, protein powder, peanut butter, honey, and cocoa powder together in a blender until smooth and your shake is ready.
