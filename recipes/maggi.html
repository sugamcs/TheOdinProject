The famous maggi noodles

Description

Maggi is easy to make pack of noodles, which are great snacks specially when you crave for something at 2am :D

It is famous among the youth in india, and i mostly preffered to be the maincourse when it comes to travelling to hilly areas where it is seen at almost all stalls


Ingredients

  • Pack of noodles
  • 300ml of water
  • Maggi masala
  • Any vegetable(optional) this dish does not necessarily need vegetables

Steps

  1. Boil the water and drop the pack of noodles in the boiling water
  2. Add the maggi masala with vegetables(optional)
  3. Let it boil for about 5 mins and keep stirring it and your maggi is ready.
